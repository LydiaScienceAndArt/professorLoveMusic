So here is a few words on what has happened in the last 2 years for two courses I have taught:
Update
E/ME 103 Management of Technology Fall 2017 and Fall 2018
This course is for students interested in learning how rapidly evolving technologies are harnessed to produce useful products, successful businesses and great research.

The concept is to analytically predict what will happen in the next ~ 5 years.

The widely varied subjects reflect the diversity of personal interests of the Student Teams!

The Final Papers are available upon request.
